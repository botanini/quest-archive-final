Essential Adventuring Gear

This collection features six key items often found in the inventories of D&D characters. From healing potions to magical weaponry, these objects provide vital support during exploration and combat.

Data: Each item is commonly found in official modules or homebrew campaigns. Healing potions and gold serve survival and economy, while spell scrolls and enchanted items like the flaming sword enhance combat versatility.

  • Healing Potion: Restores hit points. Potion comes in different levels restoring more hitpoints the better the potion is.
  • Bag of Gold: Currency for transactions. Currency comes in copper pieces (cp), silver pieces (sp), electrum pieces (ep), gold pieces (gp), and platinum pieces (pp)
  • Map: Navigation and exploration aid. This can be a visual for the players and the DM to know where they are traveling to and from.
  • Fireball Spell: Area damage spell. Every Wizard's Favorite Spell. The fan favorite. I mean who doesn't love a large ball of fire that does a lot of damage?
  • Flaming Sword: Magical weapon with fire damage. Similar to the Fireball, very common magical item that you can never really have any problems with I mean it just adds damage.
  • D&D Dice: Essential for gameplay mechanics. Experience watching your dice make or break the entire story.
