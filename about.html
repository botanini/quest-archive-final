About the Team

Our Quest Archive

Into the Dungeon began in early 2025 as a creative capstone project by a group of D&D fans who wanted to make an accessible, visually rich resource for new and veteran players alike. What started as a digital magazine featuring dice reviews and class summaries quickly evolved into a collaborative archive of community creativity, player profiles, and campaign tools.

From our humble beginnings sketching potion bottles and character sheets, we've grown into a small but dedicated team curating original content, visual guides, and roleplay inspiration for fellow adventurers across the internet. Our timeline below highlights some major milestones in the development of this project:

  • January 2025: Concept and team formed at a D&D session over pizza.
  • February 2025: Logo design and first gallery photos completed.
  • March 2025: First review drafts published and style guide finalized.
  • April 2025: Site prototype launched with five main sections and over 20 original illustrations.

Contributor Profiles

Inator Lu

Editor-in-Chief & Designer

Inator is a long-time D&D player and digital storyteller who created "Into the Dungeon" as a space to celebrate the creativity of tabletop culture. He manages site content, visual design, and brings both personal experience and fan perspectives to every section.

Ry Zotew

Lore Archivist & Review Writer

Ry is an scholar specializing in magical history and spell mechanics. Their deep dives into fantasy lore and class mechanics help inform the site's content and community engagement through feature reviews and campaign breakdowns.

Liy Ang

Illustrator & Media Specialist

Liy, art director, is responsible for most of the original illustrations and visual theming across the site. Their mixed-media sketches bring each class and campaign item to life with color and energy.
